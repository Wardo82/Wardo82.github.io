Introduction

Leverage AR technology to find the room you are looking for.

A project born in the Master Praktikum in Networked Multimedia Systems. Using a set of web frameworks for camera scanning and 3D rendering, we developed a browser based app to ease the hassle of finding your way through the TUM buildings.

Click on Navigate to access the camera and point to a QR Code around you. Then your only task is to follow the arrow to your destination.
